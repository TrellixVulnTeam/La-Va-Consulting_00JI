    The Abstract

    Lava-Consulting is a German-Peruvian based IT-, healthcare and life-science consulting and solutions company striving to provide healthcare professionals with the solutions they deserve and need. Safe&Surg is our newest product which provides surgery departments as well as hospitals with a quick, flexible and customizable solution to document surgeries. In addition to standard functionality it also provides safety features like a mandatory filling out of the WHO Surgery Safety Checklist and educational ressources for junior doctors. All this is provided with 24 h customer support, guaranteed safety and the chance to deploy the system within hours all over the world - be it in previously not digitalized hospitals or crisis regions.


    The Background

    After first successfull projects Lava-Consulting is now considered an uprising star in the European IT and healthcare sky. Founded by the renowned Peruvian IT and nursing specialist Susanna Vanessa Cárdenas García and the German researching doctor turned entrepreneur Lars Masanneck LaVa Consulting now provides highend healthcare applications and consulting services in more than 50 countries. The most successful products include sophisticated nursing robots rolled out with industry partners, pandemic tracking tools implemented for the WHO and the German RKI and modern applications to document healthcare services typeles using machine learning. Due to LaVa Consulting's close relationships to the Hasso-Plattner-Institute in Potsdam and its Digital Health center our work has resulted in countless contributions to academic research.

    Safe&Surg is our newest project which aims to provide especially previously not digitalized hospitals and surgery departments with an easy solution to implement internationally recognized standards. Not only can a high level of digitalization and documentation be implemented overnight but also can the reports and pictures in the surgery files be accessed by junior doctors of the same department for educational purposes. By this we hope to support the training of young medical professionals who do have more than enough to think about already. Safe&Surg thus aims to provide modern infrastructure in previously struggeling hospitals without the usual financial burden of implementing a whole hospital information system.
